🛌 Foundational Health Habits

Quality sleep, stress management, movement, and nutrient-rich meals are essential to immune function. We help you build these core habits into your lifestyle to lower inflammation and enhance immunity.

🧃 Immune-Supportive Supplements

We recommend science-backed supplements such as vitamin D, zinc, quercetin, elderberry, and medicinal mushrooms like reishi and beta-glucans. These help your body defend against pathogens and recover faster.

🧬 Immune-Modulating Peptides

Peptides like Thymosin Alpha-1, LL-37, and BPC-157 are used to strengthen immune tolerance, regulate cytokine response, and support tissue repair. These are prescribed based on clinical need and lab markers.

🧪 Functional Immune Lab Testing

We assess key immune markers such as white blood cell differentials, CRP, vitamin and mineral levels, and cytokine panels. This allows us to identify gaps and fine-tune your protocol for optimal immune readiness.

🔁 Long-Term Immune Resilience

Your immune system is dynamic. We adjust your protocol over time based on exposures, seasons, travel, and stress. Whether you’re managing autoimmunity, recovering from illness, or seeking prevention, we personalize your care every step of the way.
