Why Supplements Matter

Supplements help correct nutrient deficiencies, support key biological systems, and accelerate results when lifestyle alone isn’t enough. But quality and personalization are key. At ABBSI, we only recommend supplements based on lab results and clinical evidence.

Professional Grade Formulations

All supplements we recommend are third-party tested, highly bioavailable, and free of unnecessary fillers. We use medical-grade brands trusted by clinicians to ensure purity, potency, and effectiveness.

Types of Supplements We Use
  • Metabolic Support: berberine, alpha-lipoic acid, inositol, chromium
  • Cognitive & Mood: L-theanine, phosphatidylserine, Rhodiola, magnesium threonate
  • Immune Health: vitamin D, zinc, quercetin, elderberry, medicinal mushrooms
  • Longevity: NMN, resveratrol, CoQ10, omega-3s
  • Skin & Hair: collagen peptides, hyaluronic acid, biotin, vitamin C
  • Recovery & Performance: creatine, BCAAs/EAAs, carnitine, taurine
Personalized Blends Based on Labs

We go beyond one-size-fits-all. Based on your blood work and wellness goals, we may create custom-compounded supplements—combining key ingredients into one capsule or powder to simplify your daily routine.

Integrated Into Every Protocol

Supplements are used in conjunction with lifestyle, peptides, and sometimes medications—not in place of them. They're selected to enhance your program outcomes whether you're focused on weight loss, cognition, aging, or immune health.

Clinically Guided & Safe

All supplements are prescribed or recommended under clinician supervision. We monitor for interactions, track symptom improvements, and adjust doses over time to make sure your protocol evolves with your progress.
