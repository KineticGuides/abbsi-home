High-quality supplements play a vital role in bridging nutritional gaps, supporting healing, and optimizing function—especially when guided by your lab results and health goals.

🔥 Metabolic & Weight Management

We may recommend ingredients like berberine, chromium, alpha-lipoic acid, and inositol to support blood sugar balance, insulin sensitivity, and healthy fat metabolism.

🧠 Cognitive Focus & Mood Regulation

To enhance mental clarity, resilience, and focus, we use compounds such as L-theanine, Rhodiola rosea, phosphatidylserine, and magnesium threonate.

🛡️ Immune System Support

Immune-enhancing options include vitamin D3, zinc, quercetin, elderberry, and medicinal mushrooms like reishi and shiitake for innate and adaptive immunity.

⏳ Longevity & Cellular Health

Supplements like NAD+ precursors (NMN or NR), resveratrol, pterostilbene, and mitochondrial cofactors are selected to support cellular energy, reduce oxidative stress, and extend healthspan.

💪 Muscle Recovery & Performance

We include supplements like creatine monohydrate, essential amino acids (EAAs), carnitine, and CoQ10 to boost performance and support recovery during strength and stamina programs.

✨ Skin, Hair & Aesthetic Wellness

Supportive supplements such as collagen peptides, hyaluronic acid, vitamin C, zinc, and biotin promote healthy skin, hair, and connective tissue integrity.

⚗️ Custom-Compounded Blends

In some cases, we offer compounded supplements tailored to your biomarker profile, combining multiple active ingredients into a single daily formulation for simplicity and potency.

🧪 Physician Oversight & Purity

All supplements are selected based on clinical evidence, third-party testing, and quality assurance standards. We avoid unnecessary fillers and ensure therapeutic dosing—guided by lab data and progress tracking.
