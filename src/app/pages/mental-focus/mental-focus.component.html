🧘‍♂️ Brain-Healthy Lifestyle Habits

We focus on optimizing sleep, circadian rhythm, hydration, movement, and mindfulness. These are foundational to enhancing focus and reducing cognitive fatigue. Lifestyle changes are tailored to your habits, profession, and brain health goals.

💊 Cognitive Support Supplements

Our team recommends evidence-backed nootropic supplements including L-theanine, Rhodiola, phosphatidylserine, Omega-3s, and magnesium threonate. These support memory, clarity, stress resilience, and neurotransmitter balance.

🧬 Nootropic Peptides

Peptides like Semax, Selank, and Dihexa may be used to enhance brain-derived neurotrophic factor (BDNF), improve attention, and support mood. These peptides are physician-guided and tailored to your cognitive needs and biochemistry.

🧠 Cognitive Medications (When Needed)

For patients with more significant cognitive complaints, attention issues, or brain fog, medications such as low-dose psychostimulants or wakefulness-promoting agents may be considered after medical evaluation and lab testing.

📈 Personalized Tracking & Adjustments

We monitor biomarkers tied to brain health (e.g., cortisol, homocysteine, nutrient levels) and track subjective improvements in focus, attention span, and mental energy. Your plan evolves as your clarity and cognitive stamina improve.
